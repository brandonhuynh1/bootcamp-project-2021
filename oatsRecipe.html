One Minute Oats

Perhaps the most convenient breakfast of the bunch, 1 minute oats!

Ingredients

  • Oats
  • Milk
  • Peanut butter
  • 1 Banana
  • Blue Berries
  • Cinnamon

Add an Ingredient!

Ingredient:

Preparation

  1. Pour oats into bowl with milk.
  2. Heat up oats and milk in microwave for 1 minute.
  3. Scoop 1 tbsp of peanut butter into bowl.
  4. Add desired ratio of blue berries and chopped bananas into oats.
  5. Season with cinnamon while mixing oats and berries.
  6. Voila, enjoy!
