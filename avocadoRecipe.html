Eggs On Avocado Toast

This tasty breakfast features the superfood, avocadoes, beneath some delicious eggs!

Ingredients

  • 1 Avocado
  • 2 Eggs
  • 2 slices of bread of your choice
  • Butter/cooking oil
  • Salt and Pepper

Preparation

  1. Toast bread in toaster, we're going to aim for a nice golden brown to contrast the avocado we'll spread on top.
  2. In a small/medium pan over medium heat, melt butter (or heat oil).
  3. Crack two eggs and cook for 3 minutes, or until whites are set. Flip and cook other side for 1 minute.
  4. Bread should be done toasting, spread avocado onto toast and season with salt and pepper.
  5. Lay cooked eggs on avocado toast.
  6. Voila, enjoy!
