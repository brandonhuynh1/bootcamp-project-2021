Breakfast Burrito

An easy on the go breakfast to take with you to class or on the road!

Ingredients

  • 2 Eggs
  • Chopped onions
  • 1 breakfast sausage
  • Spinach
  • Butter/cooking oil
  • 1 Tortilla
  • Salt and Pepper

Preparation

  1. In a small/medium pan over medium heat, melt butter (or heat oil).
  2. Add chopped onions and chopped breakfast sausage to pan, cooking them for 5-7 minutes, or until they begin to brown
  3. Crack 2 eggs into separate bowl, scrambling and seasoning with salt and pepper
  4. Once onions and sausage are browned, add scrambled eggs.
  5. Cook scrambled eggs for 2-3 minutes and add spinach. Continue scrambling until spinach is cooked down.
  6. Add scramble onto tortilla and roll!
  7. Voila, enjoy!
